As of Dec 31, 2016, Instinct has been permenantly shut down.

Instinct began as a labor of love by by two musicians. We raised money for it as a startup, and then it became a labor of love again when we couldn't make the business work. As the years went by the software aged and we just didn't have the time or resources to give it the attention it deserved.

Thanks so much for all the love and support over the years. We say goodbye with a heavy heart and a lot of gratitude!

All the best,
Blake & Brian
